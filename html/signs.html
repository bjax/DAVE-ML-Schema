6.5.5. Common sign convention notation

A convention for indicating positive sign conventions for measurements is included in Annex A of the draft AIAA Flight Dynamics Model Exchange Standard [AIAA11]. These acronyms, if applicable, should be used whenever possible to enhance communications.
